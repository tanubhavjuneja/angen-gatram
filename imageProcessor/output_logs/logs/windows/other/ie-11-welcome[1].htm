A new privacy setting. Websites you visit receive a Do Not Track request when you use Internet Explorer. To learn more about this setting, including how to turn it off, see Use Do Not Track in Internet Explorer.

A note about your settings. If you upgraded from an earlier version of Internet Explorer, your settings for SmartScreen Filter, Suggested Sites, and Compatibility Lists Updates have been saved, unless you were prompted to decide again. To improve your browsing experience, some limited additional data is collected, Compatibility Lists now update more compatibility settings, and search suggestions are available when you search from the address bar. For more info, including how to change previously configured settings, see the Internet Explorer privacy statement.
