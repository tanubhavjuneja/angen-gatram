How to Disappear Forever

By an eHow Contributor
  • Share
  • Print this article

If you've ever felt like dropping off the face of the earth, to get away from everything without harming yourself, there are methods you can employ. You can disappear without leaving a trace.

Other People Are Reading

  • How to Change Your Own Identity
  • How to Go Into Hiding

Instructions

    • 1

      Leave your present locale. Don't say goodbye. Don't have mail forwarded. Simply vanish forever.

    • 2

      Change your name. You'll leave a record of this name change, so do it from your new location. The smaller your new location is, the less likely anyone will find the record or you.

    • 3

      Lay low in your new location. It's hard to get a job or bank account because these things require you to give your social security number which then traces you to your present place. You can do odd jobs, though, and keep your money at home.

    • 4

      Enter the Federal Witness Security program to disappear forever if your situation warrants it. They provide you with a new identity, complete with new social security number and name.

    • 5

      Check to see if you can legally change your social security number if your situation doesn't warrant witness protection, but you're in a perilous situation. For example, if your former spouse is threatening or stalking you, you may be able to change your name, location and social security legally to vanish forever.

    • 6

      Rough it. Go off in the wilds, perhaps even in a foreign country, and live off the land. You won't have any phone bills, mortgages, bank accounts, tax forms or other ties that can lead people to you.
